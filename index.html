How It Works

Four steps, fully automatic.

CommPilot runs quietly in the background — from inbox to response — without requiring any action from your staff.

01

Monitor

CommPilot connects securely to your organization's inbox and watches for new incoming inquiries continuously — day and night.

02

Analyze

An AI model reads and understands the email, classifying the inquiry type and identifying exactly what the person needs to know.

03

Generate

A second AI model crafts a warm, accurate, personalized response using your organization's approved information and voice.

04

Respond & Log

The reply goes out from your email address. The full interaction is logged for review, compliance, and continuous improvement.

Privacy & Control

Your data never leaves your building.

CommPilot runs entirely on your own infrastructure using local AI models. No email content, no personally identifiable information, and no organizational data is ever transmitted to a third-party AI service.

✓

Local AI processing

All classification and response generation happens on your servers using proven open-source models — never on cloud AI platforms.

✓

Complete audit trail

Every interaction is logged in a secure local database. Review, export, or report on any communication at any time.

✓

FERPA-compatible architecture

Designed with educational data privacy in mind. Student and family information stays within your controlled environment.
